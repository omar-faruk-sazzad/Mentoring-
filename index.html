About

Not a coach.
Something closer.

I'm not here to give you a five-step plan. I'm here to sit with you in the mess — and think through it clearly, honestly, and without judgment.

I'm Omar Faruk Sazzad — founder of PsychTalks and Batchit, two platforms built on the conviction that psychological insight should be accessible, not locked behind jargon or privilege.

I study people. I write about them. In these sessions, I think with them. My background is a blend of psychology, philosophy, organizational thinking, and the kind of lived confusion that either breaks you or makes you more precise.

I chose precision. These sessions are for those ready to do the same.

Why This Exists

Most people have
no one to
really talk to.

Not because they're alone — but because the people around them aren't always safe to be honest with. Family has expectations. Friends have opinions. And therapists cost what most people can't afford.

So the questions stay inside. The confusion grows. The same patterns repeat.

This is meant to be that space. A place where you can say the thing you've been carrying — and someone will listen without judgment, think with you without agenda, and help you find your own clarity.

No performance. No pretense. Just an honest conversation in a space that's genuinely safe.

I
A judgment-free space

Nothing you say here will be used against you. You can be confused, contradictory, or lost — that's exactly the right place to start.

II
Psychologically grounded

Rooted in real frameworks — attachment theory, cognitive patterns, identity development — not inspirational guesswork.

III
Locally rooted

Built for the Bangladeshi context — familiar with the pressures, the expectations, and the silence around emotional life here.

IV
You find your own answer

I won't tell you what to do. But I'll help you think clearly enough to know it yourself.
